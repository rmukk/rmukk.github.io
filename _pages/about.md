I'm a 2nd-year chemical engineering PhD candidate at Caltech, co-advised by [Prof. Julia Kornfield](https://kornfield.caltech.edu/) and [Prof. Dianne Newman](https://dknweb.caltech.edu/). My research blends polymer science with microbiology to develop living materials for precision agriculture applications. 

I completed my BSE in Chemical Engineering at Case Western Reserve University (CWRU) in 2022. During my undergraduate studies, I worked on developing polyolefin blends suitable for scalable conversion to carbon fibers in [Prof. Valentin Rodionov's](https://rodionovlab.org/) polymer chemistry group and on simulating the dynamics of bottle-brush polymers in [Prof. Michael J.A. Hore's](https://www.horegroup.com/michael-j-a-hore-phd) polymer physics group. 

Outside of the lab, I enjoy playing tennis, hiking, and competing in mixed martial arts.
